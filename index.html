Our Mission

The Khine Lab Team is dedicated to improving human health by developing innovative, low cost, and scalable point of care and continuous monitoring solutions. Because our platform technology originated with an eureka moment and a children's toy, we are also committed to 'pay it forward' by helping to invent the next generation of inventors. We want to change the world by developing solutions to improve and democratize healthcare and education.
